My YouTube Journey

My YouTube Channel was created on July 6, 2016

My YouTube Channel

My first YouTube video was uploaded to YouTube on August 14, 2016

This was a tutorial video about how signup for PlayStation Network on PS3

To this day, this is my most popular video, being at 367,420 views

Did some videos on Call of Duty Black Ops

Then Eventually went to Minecraft Videos

And made some videos about Kahoot! and Class Dojo for some reason

On January 20, 2017, I reached my first 100 subscribers

Then in Feburary 19, 2017, I made my first YouTube series known as Battle for Million Dollars, which is basically a Minecraft version of BFDI.

Little did I know, this would become one of my most successful series on YouTube

The first Season came to a close on December 16, 2017, with a total of 24 episodes

The second season of BFMD, came out four days after Season 1 ended continuning off where Season 1 left off at

Season 2 was a lot shorter than Season 1, with only having 11 episodes

Season 2 was concluded on January 3, 2018

There are seasons 3, 4, and 5, but those were started but never finished.

In March of 2019, I launched a new series known as Wabagg's Astral Battle Royale Experience which is about a boy named Wabagg Wilson, who goes to sleep one night and accidently astral projects into another world that looks exactly like the real world, but somethings are different.

Season 1 of the series lasted for about a year, ending on July 29, 2020

On August 24, 2019, I had reached my first 1,000 subscribers.

On September of 2020, I launched another series, which kind of connects to Wabagg's Astral Battle Royale Experience and Battle for Million Dollars. This series was known as the Z God Series and it's about how each series has it's own universe. Like there's a Wabagg Wilson Universe, BFMD Universe, and others that I may have created. I even wind up going to one of these universes.

The first season of the series ended on April 30, 2021, which goes back into Wabagg Wilson's universe for Season 2 of that Series

On September 20, 2021, the first episode of Season 2 of Wabagg's Astral Battle Royale Experience dropped.

The series was concluded on June 15, 2022 and that was the last series I made for my channel

Around in May of 2022, I sort of started watching My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic! and I became obsseed with it.

I also liked the game, Friday Night Funkin, around that time

So during mostly from 2022-2023, I made FNF My Little Pony videos and those actually got a decent amount of views and likes

I've also worked on a few Wii Shop revivals and some other related Wii stuff around this time, which I made videos about.

From 2024-Now, I haven't really made too much content lately, but I am working on something big, which I hope everyone will enjoy. I can't talk about that yet though
